Project Overview

MiningChecklistApp safety & tracking mobile app

A semester project focused on supporting mining/engineering workflows through digital checklists, task tracking, and safety-related reporting.

!

Problem

Inspection logs can fail or get lost when equipment checklists are paper-based, slow, or not synced to a central dashboard.

S

Solution

MiningChecklistApp centralises active checklists, shift logs, emergency reporting, and Firebase database storage into a real-time mobile app.

U

Users

Mining engineers, site technicians, safety inspectors, supervisors, and shift leads.

I

Impact

The app ensures equipment checks and shift handovers are digitally logged, improving compliance and emergency response times.
